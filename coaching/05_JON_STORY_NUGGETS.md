## The weekly check-in template (a proven structure the app can mirror)

Jon's ~15 sheets all follow one format — essentially a weekly-review OS:
- **Request for coaching this week** (the one thing to work on)
- **Practices completed / incomplete**
- **Projects 1–4:** *What by When* · *Next Milestone* · *Update/Progress this week*
- **Number of clients** (pro-bono / partial / full-pay) · **Coaching hours to date** · **MOPAs in progress / completed**

*App relevance:* this is a real-world weekly-review schema — a natural template for the MOPA engine
([04](04_INTEGRATION_ROADMAP.md) #8) and a "weekly check-in" companion to the daily ritual.

## Jon's real arc (2016, drawn from his check-in logs)

A ~6-month transformation captured week by week — victim/guarded → responsible/scaling → completing.

- **Summer:** hard-charging sales (tracking ~62 → 75 toward a 125-accounts goal), working weekends. Wants
  "an awesome beautiful girlfriend by January." Chasing coaching certification (100+ logged hours, 9 clients).
  *Coaching request: "being one with the arrogance"* — naming his ego/guardedness shadow.
- **Early fall:** requests shift to **connection** ("on relationships doing what's convenient", "connecting to
  others"). A stall — pro-bono clients drop to 0, MOPAs pause. A real overwhelm dip.
- **October:** deeper relational work — *"rate my last 3 relationships,"* where he's confronted, how he
  **sabotages relationships**. Scales sales by **hiring appointment setters / building a team**. New project:
  **home renos**. "Been putting myself out more on meeting people."
- **November (Sheet 33):** the vulnerable turn — *"getting complete with having herpes."* Building a team
  (5 hired → target 10) to sell 100+ accounts/month. New project: **start a non-profit** (procrastinating,
  *"this feels like allot to handle"*). Cert pushed to May. *Essence conversation with Ben.*
- **November (Sheet 36):** integration — team of 10 hired, car-payoff goal, cert setup call with Murray,
  renos progressing (buying carpet), *"getting complete with the coaching program."*

## Coach-business docs — transmuted into end-user gems

The app is **not** teaching anyone to coach. But the *human move* under each coach-business doc transfers:

| Source (coach-business) | Transmuted end-user gem | Where it could live |
|---|---|---|
| Handling Objections | Surface & move **your own inner objections** — "what's in the way? if it were gone, what's possible?" | BUFCA variant / Possibility prompt |
