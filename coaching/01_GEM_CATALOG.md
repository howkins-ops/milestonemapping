## ⭐ The through-line: BUFCA

Jon's steer: **BUFCA is the master move — the concept under all the others.** Almost every tool here is a
specialized way of doing one thing: *take a stuck/triggered moment and convert it into committed action
from essence instead of reaction from the survival mechanism.* BUFCA is that move in five clean steps, so
it becomes the **centerpiece of the Shadow area** and the pattern the other exercises plug into.

### BUFCA — From Breakdown to Action (Weekend 4) — **NEW ⭐**
- **Essence:** A 5-step escape hatch from any breakdown. It separates *upset* (story/feelings) from *facts*,
  then re-anchors on *commitment* (independent of circumstances) and the *next action*.
- **Steps (verbatim prompts):**
  - **B — Breakdown:** "What shouldn't be?"
  - **U — Upset:** "What are the thoughts, feelings, and body sensations happening right now?"
  - **F — Facts:** "What would an impartial third party (news reporter / police officer) say happened?"
  - **C — Commitment:** "Separate from the circumstances, what is your commitment in this area?"
  - **A — Action:** "Looking only at the commitment, not the circumstances or history, what's the next appropriate action?" → then take it or promise a by-when.
  - *Note from source:* self-coaching is weak — have someone read you the questions in order; if stuck, back up one step.
- **App potential:** Flagship Shadow-Forge tool. Guided 5-card flow; the "someone reads the questions" role is played by the app. The other Shadow tools become themed BUFCA variants (anger, shame, comparison…). See [04](04_INTEGRATION_ROADMAP.md).

## Foundational frameworks

### Survival Mechanism vs. Essence (Weekend 1) — DONE
- **Essence:** Two ways of being — Survival Mechanism (fear, control, reactive, victim) vs. Essence
  (present, grounded, powerful, effortless), compared across ~20 dimensions.
- **App potential:** A self-rating "which mode am I in?" indicator. *(Already the backbone of Shadow Forge / 5 masks + essences — don't rebuild.)*

### Transformation — the 5-stage model (Weekend 1) — PARTIAL
- **Essence:** (1) distinguish SM from essence → (2) manage the SM → (3) accept/allow/discover relationship
  from essence → (4) practice living from essence → (5) live from essence.
- **App potential:** A journey/progress spine — show the user which stage they're operating from over time.

### Insight → Action → Results (Weekend 4) — NEW
- **Essence:** Insight alone changes nothing; **Insight + Action = Results.** Understanding is the start, not the finish.
- **App potential:** After any reflection/insight capture, force a "so what will you *do*?" action step + completion tracking.

## Completion & state-shift tools (the heart of the un-mined toolkit)

### Three-Letter Series (Weekend 4) — NEW
- **Essence:** Move from raw emotion → responsibility → acknowledgement via three unsent letters, then destroy them.
- **Steps:** L1 *Emotions* — let it all fly, full victim, until nothing's left. L2 *Responsibility* — what I did/didn't do/say/hear that had it go this way; apologies. L3 *Acknowledgement* — honor the person, gratitude. **Never send; destroy after.**
- **App potential:** Private 3-phase guided writing tool (timer per phase, one-tap "burn" animation). A completion-flavored BUFCA.

### Responsibility-Completion — the 29 questions (Weekend 4) — NEW
- **Essence:** Reclaim power by taking 100% responsibility for how a situation went — across said/heard/saw/thought/felt/did/was, plus the right/wrong & win-lose games — ending in a completion declaration.
- **Steps:** Answer all 29 in writing (verbatim list captured in agent output / to embed at build), closing with "I am complete!"
- **App potential:** Deep-dive workbook; questions one at a time; gentle "go deeper" nudge on thin answers.

### Completion — What/When/How (Weekend 4) — NEW
- **Essence:** Completion = releasing burdensome energy and allowing things to be exactly as they are/aren't ("no more energy than last Wednesday's lunch"). Practiced, declared in the moment: "I declare this complete."
- **Steps:** Start on a low-gradient item; check whether the declaration actually *lands*; listen for false "I'm fine with that."
- **App potential:** A "Completion" entry point that routes to Three-Letter or the 29 questions, then a declaration + check-in.

### Back to Being (Weekend 4) — PARTIAL (extend Grounding)
- **Essence:** When hooked/scared you're in the SM; return to essence fast via a menu of practices (nature, breath, meditate, serve, get acknowledged, allow what is). Goal: shorten time-to-return over time.
- **App potential:** Expand `shadow/Grounding.jsx` into a "Back to Being" quick-menu + a *time-to-return* metric.

### Present Context (Weekend 3) — NEW
- **Essence:** Make the invisible current context visible to break automaticity.
- **Steps (fields):** Name · Absence of · Committed to · and how you're able to: Suffer / Settle / Survive / Resist / Avoid / Deny.
- **App potential:** A short diagnostic journal that seeds coaching/BUFCA.

## Identity, declaration & choice

### Naming the Survival Mechanism (Weekend 2) — DONE
- **Essence:** Externalize the fear-pattern as a named "character" to gain power over it. *(Already the ShadowAlchemist exercise.)*

### Declaration as a Tool (Weekend 3) — DONE (deepen)
- **Essence:** A public, time-bound statement that *causes* possibility (vs. affirmation, which overlays the SM). Power is in the speaking + action plan.
- **App potential:** Already in Ch5 + daily stands; could add a "declare → post → re-declare → obstacle-reframe" loop.

### Powerful Stands (Weekend 3) — DONE (expandable) — 34 verbatim in [03](03_QUESTION_BANKS.md)
- **Essence:** Short "I am…" possibility declarations that rewire default being ("I am my essence", "I am already worthy", "I accept, allow, and discover").
- **App potential:** The app has 6 stand themes — fold in the 34-item source list as more options / a "stand of the day."

### Choice — Three Options (Weekend 5) — NEW
- **Essence:** Only three relationships to a choice — empowering, disempowering, or *no choice* (which defaults to disempowering). Naming "no choice as a choice" reveals its cost.
- **App potential:** A decision micro-prompt: name the 3 options, name which you're actually taking.

### Opposite Of — As a Tool (Weekend 10) — NEW
- **Essence:** The SM usually points away from growth; do the *opposite* of what it urges.
- **App potential:** Daily prompt — "what's your SM telling you to do? → do the opposite" → evening "what happened?".

### Bankrupt & Red-Flag Words (Weekend 6) — NEW
- **Essence:** Certain words signal the SM: *willpower, happy, hard, overwhelm, hope, try, fair, want, stop, why.* Swapping them shifts victim→creator (try→do, want→commit, hope→generate).
- **App potential:** A "language coach" that flags red-flag words in journal entries and suggests power swaps; weekly word-swap challenge.

### Powerful Ways To Be With People (Weekend 5) — NEW
- **Essence:** Two places to come from — fear or love. Three moves: **Get them** (listen fully), **Coach them** (only if invited), **Leave them alone** (release fixing).
- **App potential:** A pre-conversation "pick your stance" selector + after-action reflection.

### Coaching from Being / from Judgment vs. Being (Weekend 3) — coach-craft
- **Essence:** Coaching from judgment/expertise diminishes; from being/love empowers. *(More coach-craft than end-user — mine the "notice judgment → shift to compassion" move for the Relationships/Judgment tool.)*

## Goal & future design

### Project Design From The Future (Weekend 3) — PARTIAL (finish the wizard)
- **Essence:** Design goals backward from a fulfilled future, with emotional + tactical scaffolding.
- **Steps:** (1) Intended Results = Objective + Measure (SMART "What by When"); (2) **Future Vision** journal entry written *from the day it's complete* (≥1 page); (3) Skills inventory (≥10); (4) Resources/people list; (5) Rewards — small/medium/large (incl. $0); (6) **Reverse** timeline (future→now); (7) Action plan *only to the first milestone*; (8) Attach rewards to milestones.
- **App potential:** The app's Formula + Milestones cover ~half; build the full wizard (future-vision writing, skills/resources capture, reverse timeline, reward tiers).

### Futurability for Objectives — 8-point audit (Weekend 3) — NEW
- **Essence:** An objective is realizable only if it's Owned, Relevant, Measurable, Achievable, Inspired, Committed, Spoken, and Coached.
- **App potential:** A "goal viability" check on any milestone — score the 8, flag weak dimensions.

### Life Purpose (Weekend 4) — DONE
- **Essence:** Purpose is a *way of being*, discovered through iterative inquiry (chase the "clunk"), not a to-do. *(Ch19.)*

### 546 Goals (Weekend 4) — NEW (content library)
- **Essence:** A 546-item brainstorm of life goals across every domain — a possibility stimulus.
- **App potential:** Browsable/collectable goals library; tag by domain; link chosen goals to projects.

### Generating Practice Areas (Weekend 4) — NEW
- **Essence:** Weekly co-created action items that translate insight into behavior (notice / choose / complete / acknowledge / take action / allow / serve…). The bridge from session to life.
- **App potential:** The core of the MOPA engine (see [04](04_INTEGRATION_ROADMAP.md)).

### Vision / Mission / Strategy / Tactics (Weekend 10) — PARTIAL
- **Essence:** Cascading intention — Vision (why, no time horizon) → Mission (what/for whom) → Strategy (finite plan + measures) → Tactics (specific moves).
- **App potential:** A personal strategic-planner ladder above milestones.

### Time Generator (root tool) — NEW
- **Essence:** Six questions to liberate time: what to stop, delegate, shorten, eliminate, bundle, or develop others to own.
- **App potential:** A time-audit mini-tool → personalized "time liberation" plan.

### Top Six Checklist (root tool) — DONE-ish (Top Five exists)
- **Essence:** Six ranked daily priorities; hammer #1, drop to #2 if blocked, do an errand to shift energy when stuck, any completion is a win, fresh list daily.
- **App potential:** The app's Top Five already covers this — optionally add the **"blocked → next → errand mode"** nuance.

### Problems as Opportunities — 6-question protocol (Weekend 8) — NEW
- **Essence:** No inherent problems, only "what is so" + interpretation. Mine each problem for opportunity.
- **Steps:** For a problem: how is this like my past? what normally happens next? what would be present if I handled it for good? how am I using it to kill possibility? what would I do if there were no problems? what actions stand between me and that life?

## Growth, energy & self-sabotage

### Re-Invention — Why/How/When (Weekend 7) — DONE-ish (Ch20)
- **Essence:** Clinging to old tools/expertise kills growth; reinvent continuously; get coached on what you can't yet handle.
- **App potential:** A stagnation detector ("no growth logged in 60 days → reinvention prompt").

### Leader Care Checklist — 20 areas (Weekend 7) — NEW
- **Essence:** Audit daily/weekly self-care *from the perspective of your future-leader self* (body, integrity, support, self-acceptance, delegation…).
- **App potential:** A daily leadership-practice tracker + monthly "how is future-me showing up?" reflection.

### Turbo Chargers & Instigators (Weekend 10) — NEW
- **Essence:** Three forces: Turbo Chargers (rest/exercise/completion/play), Breakdown Instigators, Breakthrough Instigators (declaring breakdowns/commitments/acting in fear). Amplify chargers, eliminate breakdowns, trigger breakthroughs on purpose.
- **App potential:** A personal energy tracker — schedule chargers, cut one breakdown, practice one breakthrough.

### Addressing Self-Sabotage — Practice Areas (Weekend 7) — NEW
- **Essence:** Concrete pattern-breakers: **No-Media week**, **Plan-Tomorrow-Today** (script the next day each night), **Bed/Rise-time promise**.
- **App potential:** Weekly challenges with daily check-ins + "what did you notice?" journaling.

### Experiential Exercises (Weekend 6) — NEW
- **Essence:** Whole-person exercises that override the SM: **Just Say Yes** (a week of yeses), **Getting No's** (make requests to be rejected), **Blind Walk/Run** (trust + full-out action), **Essence Closet** (dress/environment from essence), **Take My Chair** (role-switch empathy).
- **App potential:** An experiential-challenge library with completion tracking + reflection (esp. Getting-No's and Essence Closet).

### Coaching on a Gradient (Weekend 6) — NEW (design principle)
- **Essence:** Breakthrough lives between too-easy (boredom) and too-steep (shutdown); calibrate pace constantly; reframe fear as proximity to possibility; celebrate wins.
- **App potential:** Adaptive difficulty across all guided practices — learn the user's threshold, tune intensity.

### Infinity Triangle (Weekend 5) — NEW
- **Essence:** Self ↔ Others ↔ Spirit/Life — power emerges when all three are honored and balanced.
- **App potential:** A visual three-vertex check-in with rebalancing suggestions.

### Turtle, Eagle, Snake (Weekend 10) — NEW
- **Essence:** Three work archetypes — Turtle (steady/detail), Eagle (visionary/leader), Snake (persuasive/unpredictable). Relate to each differently.
- **App potential:** A team/relationship archetype mapper with tailored approach tips.

## NEW PILLAR — Relationships (Weekend 9)

### Couples "I" Exercise — NEW ⭐
- **Essence:** ~70 structured "I ___" prompts (I'm scared / resent / appreciate / forgive / want / am committed to… ending in "What I love about you"), each partner answering, ~60–90 min. Repairs stuck relating fast.
- **App potential:** A guided couples flow — prompt cards, per-prompt timer, "switch who goes first" markers, optional solo/journaling mode. (Full prompt list captured for build.)

### Four Corners of Relationship — NEW
- **Essence:** Relationships rest on **Relatedness · Responsibility · Integrity · Communication**; weakness in any corner weakens the whole.
- **App potential:** A quad-chart self-audit → one practice for the weakest corner.

### Men Need / Women Need — NEW
- **Essence:** Primary receive-needs — Men: Trust, Acceptance, Appreciation, Admiration, Approval, Encouragement · Women: Caring, Understanding, Respect, Devotion, Validation, Reassurance.
- **App potential:** A needs assessment + weekly "practice giving [need]" tracker.

### Win-Win Agreement — NEW
- **Essence:** Any collaboration needs explicit Desired Results, Guidelines, Timelines, Resources, Accountability, Consequences.
- **App potential:** A fill-in agreement builder (partner/co-parent/colleague) with checkpoint reminders.

### My Pattern in Relationship · Conditions of Satisfaction — NEW
- **App potential:** Pattern-spotting journal + an explicit "what would satisfaction look like?" template (also feeds Milestones).
