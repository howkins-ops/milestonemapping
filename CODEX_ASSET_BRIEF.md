## UNIVERSAL STYLE ANCHOR
> Apply this style to EVERY image unless the prompt says otherwise.
>
> **This app is about personal transformation, momentum, and winning at life. The visuals must feel UPLIFTING and ENERGETIC — not dark, not gothic, not foreboding.**
>
> ### MORNING / BREAKTHROUGH MODE (day-facing content, backgrounds, badges, celebrations)
> **Visual language:** Cinematic personal development energy. Dawn breaking. The moment everything changes.
> **Color palette:** Deep warm navy/indigo → electric gold (#FACC15) → blinding white light. Warm amber and cyan contrast. God-rays.
> **Mood:** Rocky running up steps at sunrise. Tony Robbins on stage. The moment you decide.
> **Key elements:** Sunrise gradients, golden god-rays, ascending light beams, dawn horizons, bold warm-to-cyan contrast, radiant warmth.
> **Feeling:** Possibility. Momentum. THIS IS YOUR DAY.
>
> ### NIGHT / ELECTRIC MODE (evening-facing content, neon energy)
> **Visual language:** Cinematic night energy. The late-night grind. Electric momentum.
> **Color palette:** Near-black base → hot magenta (#FF006E) → volt cyan (#00F0FF) → electric white highlights.
> **Mood:** Night run with music blasting. Neon city. Electric and alive.
> **Key elements:** Neon trails, electric sparks, pulse waves, bold color contrast, kinetic motion blur, vibrant light against dark.
> **Feeling:** Unstoppable. Electric. You own the night.
>
> ### ICONS & BADGES (both modes, keep consistent)
> Clean geometric. Bold. Neon glowing. Sharp and iconic. Primary cyan (#00F0FF) with gold (#FACC15) or magenta (#FF006E) accents. Never muddy or muted.
>
> ### CRITICAL RULES (ALL IMAGES)
> - NO text. NO UI panels. NO sidebars. NO labels. NO interface elements of any kind.
> - NO dark gothic fog. NO dim crystals shrouded in darkness. NO haunted/foreboding energy.
> - Images should make you feel PUMPED UP and ready to conquer the day — not like you're in a dungeon.
> - Pure visual art only. Transparent background where noted (PNG).
> - Cinematic quality. Bold contrast. Never muddy or washed out.
