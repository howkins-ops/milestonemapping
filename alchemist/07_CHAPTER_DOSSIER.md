## The Business-mentor sales reservoir (deep teaching; feeds Ch4/6/8/13 + optional side-lessons)
- **Law of Probability:** track approaches → presentations → closes; ~97% "no" is the *math*, not a verdict.
- **Rapport — the Rock Collection:** "forget what I'm selling — tell me about these rocks" → she bought because
  he cared about *her*. Take a genuine interest first.
- **"No means Know":** objections = not enough info yet ("too expensive" = "tell me more about the value").
- **Listen to understand, not to reply** (Covey); tonality/body language is 70–93% of the message.
- **Empathy, not sympathy:** closers empathize and still close; sorry salespeople sympathize and lose.
- **Value must exceed price:** re-stack value; never just drop the price.
- **Enthusiasm = 50% of the sale** (en-theos, "God within"); "transfer of enthusiasm" (Tracy).
- **Mastery formula:** Knowledge + Practice = Skills; Skills + Time × Consistency = Mastery.
- **Choose the right product/org:** own what you sell; pick a place where *hundreds* win, not one or two.
- **Fill-Your-Cup:** can't pour from an empty cup; the Hour of Power; 5 ways to fill the cup → ties to the
  app's existing Daily / Fill Cup tabs.
