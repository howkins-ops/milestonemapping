★ The whole point of the ending

Chapter 1 you "Create Your Why" — but it's only the seed. The lesson is deliberately hidden. You think you're chasing a goal.

Chapter 19 — The Treasure Mirror is the climax: you open the treasure, see a mirror, and realize the Life Purpose was what you were looking for the whole time — you always had it. It only works because Chapter 1's answer was saved and held back until now.
